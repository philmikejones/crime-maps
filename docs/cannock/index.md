# Crimes in Cannock South and West

- Crimes recorded in [Cannock South and West](https://www.police.uk/pu/your-area/staffordshire-police/cannock-south-and-west/) neighbourhood

- Maps for all recorded crime types published by `data.police.uk`
- Boundary area is `WV4` neighbhourood (see https://data.police.uk/data/boundaries/)
